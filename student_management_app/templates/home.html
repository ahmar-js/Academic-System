About Us

Empowering students to achieve their dreams of joining prestigious cadet colleges through comprehensive and personalized education.

  • Our program covers all the key topics and skills needed to succeed on the cadet college entry test.
  • Our team of experienced educators and military professionals provide personalized attention and guidance to each student.
  • Our graduates have a proven track record of success, with many securing places at top cadet colleges.

Our students leave the program feeling confident, motivated, and ready to tackle any challenge.

Why Choose Us?

Our core values include excellence, integrity, respect, accountability, teamwork, and innovation. These values guide everything we do, from our curriculum to our extracurricular activities. We believe that a well-rounded education, focusing on academic excellence, character-building, leadership, and physical fitness, is essential for success in all aspects of life.

Our Vision

Our mission is to provide exceptional education and training to students aspiring to join the armed forces and other esteemed professions. We believe that every student has the potential to achieve great things.

Our History

Highbrows Forces School and Academy was established in 2000 with a vision to provide top-notch education to students aspiring to join the prestigious cadet colleges of the country.

Continue Growing

We strive to maintain the highest standards of excellence in all that we do, and we are confident that our students will continue to achieve great success in all aspects of their lives.
